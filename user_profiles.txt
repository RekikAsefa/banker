User profiles:-

1.Business Customers:

Individuals within the business customer's organization who need access to SWIFT transfer services.
May have varying levels of permissions and roles based on their responsibilities.


2.Customer Service Representatives:

Employees responsible for processing SWIFT transfer requests within the CRM module.
Handle request validations, approvals, and address customer queries.
Interact with both the web module and CRM for request processing.


3.CRM Administrators:

- Create customer server representatives users with approprate permissions
- Manage and configure settings within the CRM module.
- Ensure the CRM is aligned with the business requirements and integrates seamlessly with the web module.
